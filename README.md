# Worldbearers

**Status: Early Work in Progress**

Worldbearers is a Hytale mod that adds colossal, living creatures to the world. These aren't just big enemies. They are moving, breathing parts of the landscape that you can climb, fight on top of, and even build on.

This mod is an entry for the [Hytale New Worlds Modding Contest](https://hytale.curseforge.com/newworldscontest/).

## What's in the Mod

Worldbearers introduces two flagship giants, a new traversal tool, and the systems that tie them together.

### The Colossus

A towering hostile giant that serves as a multi-phase boss encounter. You won't beat this one by standing on the ground and swinging a sword. When the Colossus slams its club into the earth, its arm goes rigid for a few seconds, giving you a window to climb. Scale its body, dodge its attempts to shake you off, and strike the weak points on its back and neck to bring it down.

The fight changes as the Colossus takes damage. Early on, attack windows are generous and its movements are predictable. As it gets more desperate, the windows shrink, new attacks come out, and you'll need to read its tells carefully to survive.

### The World Turtle

A gentle, mountain-sized turtle that roams the world with an entire ecosystem growing on its shell. Grass, trees, pools of water, and small creatures all live on its back. You can climb aboard and build a home up there. Blocks you place on the turtle move with it as it walks, so your base travels with the creature wherever it goes.

### The Grappling Hook

A new tool that lets you fire a hook at distant surfaces and pull yourself toward them, or swing from attachment points on the giants' bodies. It works throughout the world, but it really shines during giant encounters where vertical movement and quick repositioning are essential.

## Current State

This mod is in active development. Many features described above are planned or partially implemented. Expect bugs, placeholder art, and incomplete systems. Updates will be published regularly as things come together.

If you run into issues or have feedback, feel free to leave a comment on the CurseForge page.

## Contest Entry

Worldbearers is an entry in the Hytale New Worlds Modding Contest, primarily in the NPCs category. The contest runs through April 28, 2026.
